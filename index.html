Email Providers of Baltic Municipalities

What is this?

A map of all ~182 municipalities in Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania showing which provider handles their official email — grouped by jurisdiction — based on public DNS records.

Digital sovereignty

US-based providers (Microsoft, Google, AWS) are subject to the US CLOUD Act, which allows US authorities to request stored data regardless of where it is hosted. This conflicts with GDPR and the Schrems II ruling. This map makes the provider landscape visible.

How does it work?

Each municipality's official domain is checked via public DNS records (MX + SPF) and classified by provider type.

Disclaimer: DNS records indicate mail routing, not necessarily where data is stored.
